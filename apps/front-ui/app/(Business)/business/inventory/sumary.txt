Summary of the Inventory Management System
This implementation delivers a comprehensive Inventory Management system that:

1. Multi-Store Inventory Tracking:
  View stock levels by store
  Set minimum stock levels
  Identify low stock and out of stock items
  Adjust inventory with reasons
2. Purchase Order Management:
  Create orders with suppliers
  Track expected delivery dates
  Mark orders as received
  View order history
3. Transfer Order Management:
  Move inventory between stores
  Track transfer status
  Confirm receipt of transferred items
  View transfer history
4. Real-time Integration:
  Inventory updates when sales are completed
  Stock adjustments reflected immediately
  Real-time notifications for order updates
  Responsive Design:
  Works on all device sizes
  Mobile-friendly for warehouse staff
  Desktop optimized for management
